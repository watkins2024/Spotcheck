Frequently Asked Questions

Do you contact guests?

No. We don’t communicate with guests and we don’t take guest calls. Communication is with owners/managers via WhatsApp or email only.

Do you make beds?

No. We can move linen to/from a central cupboard, but we don’t make beds.

Are you insured?

Yes — fully insured. See our insurance certificate.

How do I pay?

Airbnb Core is billed monthly in advance on the 1st. Add-ons and emergency call-outs are charged at month end.

How is scheduling handled?

We run a circuit Tue–Thu 5–9pm. Your visit happens within that window; we’ll advise any changes in advance.

Do you clean?

No — we’re caretaking: security, readiness and reporting. We do quick exterior tidy (sweep/cobwebs) and visual cleaning checks. We also do resupplies, emergency one off visits and a little extra every time according to your wishes
